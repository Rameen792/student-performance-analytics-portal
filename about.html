Our Mission

EduAnalytics was built to help teachers, coordinators, and school administrators replace scattered spreadsheets with a single, reliable platform for tracking academic performance across classes, subjects, and terms.

We believe that clear data leads to better decisions — and better decisions lead to better outcomes for every student.
